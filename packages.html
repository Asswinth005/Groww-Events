FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize a package for my team?
Absolutely! Every package can be customized to meet your team's specific needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll create a tailored experience.
What's your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 14 days before the event. 50% refund for cancellations within 7-14 days. No refund for cancellations within 7 days, but you can reschedule.
Do you offer virtual team building?
Yes! All our packages are available in virtual format. We use Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet with interactive features to ensure engagement.
Can you host events at our location?
Absolutely. We can come to your office, a venue of your choice, or recommend suitable locations. For large events, we can also arrange venues.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer a 15% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact us with your credentials.
