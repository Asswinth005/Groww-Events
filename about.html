Our Story

It started with a conversation at a remote team retreat. Our founder, Michael, watched as colleagues sat in silence during breaks, scrolling through phones instead of connecting with each other.

That moment sparked an idea: what if we could design experiences that actually bring people together? Not forced fun, but authentic moments of collaboration and discovery.

"In 2020, EVENTRO was born with a simple mission: help teams build real connections through meaningful experiences."

MW

Michael Wong

Founder & CEO

What drives us

Our core values

These principles guide every event we design and every interaction we have.

Authenticity

No cheesy trust falls. We create real moments that translate to better workplace relationships.

Customization

Your team's culture is unique—your team building should be too. Every event is tailored.

Growth mindset

We focus on learning and development, helping teams communicate better and solve problems together.

Inclusivity

Activities that work for all cultures, backgrounds, and abilities—everyone feels welcome.

Sustainability

We minimize waste and offer virtual options to reduce carbon footprint without sacrificing fun.

Fun, always

Because when people enjoy themselves, they open up, connect, and remember the experience.
