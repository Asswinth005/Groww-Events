Questions?

Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, but we can often accommodate last-minute requests—just contact us!
What if my team is fully remote?
We have a wide variety of virtual activities designed for remote teams—escape rooms, trivia, cooking classes, and more.
Is there a deposit required?
Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your date. The balance is due after the event.
Can I combine multiple activities?
Absolutely! Most packages include 2-4 activities. You can mix and match from our catalog.
What if we have more than 100 people?
We specialize in large-scale events. Contact us for a custom quote—we can handle teams of any size.
